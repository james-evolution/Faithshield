Wait...

Wait.

You don’t actually want to be here, do you?

Take a moment to reconsider. You know what this leads to.

Regret, fear, shame, disappointment, depression.

Don't do this to yourself. Be with family. Give attention to your pets. Watch a movie. Play a game. Get away from technology. Pray.

Remember your reasons for being better.

Every temptation is an opportunity to do good. Much is promised to those who resist. A few seconds of fake pleasure is not worth more than the blessings you would otherwise receive. Sin is deceitful. Its pleasure lasts for but a moment, then the hole is filled with regret. God's blessings bring true joy, and that joy lasts.

"Blessed is the man that endureth temptation: for when he is tried, he shall receive the crown of life, which the Lord hath promised to them that love him." - James 1:12

"There hath no temptation taken you but such as is common to man: but God is faithful, who will not suffer you to be tempted above that ye are able; but will with the temptation also make a way to escape, that ye may be able to bear it." - 1 Corinthians 10:13

"Jesus answered and said unto her, Whosoever drinketh of this water shall thirst again: But whosoever drinketh of the water that I shall give him shall never thirst; but the water that I shall give him shall be in him a well of water springing up into everlasting life." - John 4:13-14

This urge will pass. Go do something else for a while. You can do this. You are stronger than you think. You are not alone. The Lord is with you.
